    how it works

    Basically this is a game with 13 cards, each card having a value.
    You have access to all the 13 cards, but you can only choose one card
    randomly(if you choose to play LUCK)
    or by testing how good you can memorize(if you choose to play
    MEMORY). When you
    choose a card, the value of that card becomes your score so far. If you
    choose another card, it adds the value
    of the current card to that of the previous one. But your cards total
    should not cross 21, if they do you have BUSTED,
    so you can't pick another card. Now if you bust or if you want to go
    ahead with the score that you have,
    its time for the dealer to play. If you are done with a round and want
    to play another round,
    just press deal.

    Win,Loses and draw Logic

  • If you bust and the dealer busts, its a draw
  • If your score is the same as the dealer's and you're both under 21, its a draw
  • If the dealer busts but you don't, you won
  • If both scores are under 21 but your score is higher than that of the dealer, you won
  • If you busts but the dealer doesn't, you lose
  • If both scores are under 21 but your score is less than that of the dealer, you lose
